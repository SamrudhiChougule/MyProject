About

Welcome to the official Astronomy and Astrophysics club of VIT and VIIT, Pune. We are a group of space enthusiasts passionate about exploring the universe, stars, galaxies, and beyond.

Notable Discoveries by Space Enthusiasts

  • William Herschel: Discovered the planet Uranus in 1781 using a telescope he built himself.

  • Clyde Tombaugh: Discovered Pluto in 1930 after years of careful observation.

  • Vera Rubin: Provided strong evidence for the existence of dark matter through galaxy rotation curves.

  • Tycho Brahe: Made precise astronomical observations that paved the way for Kepler’s laws of planetary motion.

  • Galileo Galilei: First to observe moons of Jupiter and phases of Venus through a telescope.

Astronomical Timeline
